We are an instrument for your discovery and personal journey for you to learn to stand stronger, better and more confidently when engaging with corporate public servant agents. So you must learn to be fluid and flexible in order to be able to protect yourself, your family, loved ones and the sanctity of the collective purpose we all serve here on earth as it applies to God’s Will, to the best of our ability, at any given moment.
